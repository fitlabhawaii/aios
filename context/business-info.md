## Organization Overview

FitLab Hawaii is a science-backed aesthetic and wellness lounge in Hilo, Hawai'i — "Hilo's Premier Aesthetic and Wellness Lounge." It was founded by Madeline Price, NP-C and Kiani Wong Kaaua, NP-C, both of whom came from high-volume healthcare environments (emergency medicine/community clinics and surgical pediatrics/obstetrics, respectively) and wanted to offer more personalized, relationship-focused care. The practice's philosophy is preventative and science-based rather than trend-driven, built on personalized consultations, patient education, and long-term relationships with clients.
