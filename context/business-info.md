## Organization Overview

**FitLab Hawaii, LLC** — a science-backed medical aesthetics & wellness clinic in Hilo, HI ("Hilo's Premier Aesthetic and Wellness Lounge"). Founded by **Madeline Price, NP-C** and **Kiani Wong Kaaua, NP-C**, both board-certified Family Nurse Practitioners from high-volume healthcare backgrounds who wanted to deliver personalized, preventative, relationship-focused care. Philosophy: science-based and prevention-first, not trend-driven. Brand tone: clean, elevated, approachable.

## Patient Acquisition & Sales (from SOPs)

- **Leads:** contact within **5 minutes**, call twice, voicemail on 2nd, then text + email; follow-up Day 2/4/7. Tracked in a lead sheet + Jane. (Ties directly to the GoTo call data now in the warehouse.)
- **Consultations:** a polished 10-step consult flow — clarify one main goal, assess, reflect back, educate, present **3 tiers** (Reset / Core / Starter), give a confident recommendation, handle objections, close. Sales-aware and relationship-first.

## Unit Economics (from cost sheets)

- **Neurotoxin:** cost ~$2.50–$4.13/unit (Xeomin $3.10, Dysport $4.13, Jeuveau $3.95, Daxxify $2.50) vs ~$13/unit retail — strong margin.
- **Esthetician facials:** ~$1,650 revenue/shift, ~$251 labor + ~$160 supplies → **~$1,239 profit/shift**, ~$4,781/mo (4 shifts).
