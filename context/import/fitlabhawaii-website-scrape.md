## Founding Story
Founded by Madeline Price and Kiani Wong Kaaua. Both came from high-volume healthcare environments (Madeline in emergency medicine and community clinics; Kiani in surgical pediatrics and obstetrics) and wanted to provide more personalized, relationship-focused care — which led them to start their own wellness practice.

## Mission & Values
"Science-based, preventative approach to wellness and aesthetic care," prioritizing long-term health over trends. Core philosophy: personalized consultations, patient education, and building lasting relationships. "Care rooted in integrity, expertise, and genuine commitment to helping you feel your best over time." Explicitly prevention-focused, not trend-driven.
